READ BEFORE SIGNING UP!

Donating person has to apply:
  • 18 to 60 years old
  • Above 45 kgs
  • Hemoglobin above 12 gm/dl have blood pressure 110-160 / 70-96 mmHg
  • Not to be pregnant, breastfeeding, and have at least 8 days since the start of the recent menstruation
  • No recent use of drugs or strong medicines (people who take strong medicine for a short period will not able to donate blood from one week up to 2 years)
  • to had a medical surgery for 2 years
  • If you have had one of the following conditions, you are unfortunately restricted to donating blood but recommended to encourage your family members, friends and loved ones to donate:
    • Cancer, heart diseases, HIV/AIDS, hepatitis B or C, hemophilia and thalassemia, diabetes, liver diseases, Polycythemia Vera, asthma, an endocrine disorder or a hormonal disorder
